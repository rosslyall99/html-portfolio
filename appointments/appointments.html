APPOINTMENTS

Appointment Types

Booking a fitting appointment is very important.

CUSTOMERS WITH APPOINTMENTS TAKE PRIORITY OVER CUSTOMERS THAT DONT!

We have various types of appointment.

Please read carefully before selecting.

Requesting the incorrect appointment will lengthen the booking process.

Consultation

At this 20 minute appointment, you will get the opportunity to have a look at the different outfits we offer for hire.

We will .

You won’t normally have the opportunity to try anything on at this appointment.

However, if there is time to do so feel free to request this.

Please be aware that the stock may not be available to try on at this appointment.

Kilt Outfit Measurement

At this 15 minute appointment we will be measuring you for your kilt hire.

You will not get the opportunity to try on the full outfit that you will be hiring.

We will get the measurements we require using the following process;

Kilt

We will try a kilt on top of your trousers to get the correct size.

We may ask you to try it on in the fitting room if necessary.

The kilt may not be the tartan that you will be hiring.

Jacket & Waistcoat

We will try a jacket and waistcoat on you to get the correct size.

The jacket may not be the color that you will be hiring.

The jacket and waistcoat come as a set, so they will be the same size.

If different sized jacket & waistcoat are required, there will be a £20 surcharge.

Shoes

You will try a pair of shoes on to get the correct size.

Our shoes are made quite big, so it is unlikely that they will match your current shoe size.

It is very important that you try these on at the appointment.

Shirt

If you require a shirt, you will try one on for size.

We offer regular, slim, and tall fitting shirts.

As we do not know your sizes when you first visit us, we cannot guarantee that you will be able to try on the outfit at this type of appointment.

To try on the full outfit we need to organise a Full Try On appointment.

Full Try On

At this 30 minute appointment, you will be trying on the full outfit you are hiring from us.

This is normally arranged following a Kilt Outfit Measurement appointment.

If this is your first visit to us we won't know your sizes, so we cannot guarantee we will have the right sizes/style available to try on.

If we don't have the correct stock available for you to try on, we will measure you as we would for a Kilt Outfit Measurement appointment,
and then organise a Full Try On appointment for a later date.

Hire Any Tartan

At this 30 minute appointment, you will be choosing the tartan that you would like to have made for your Hire Outfit.

You will be measured up for the rest of your Kilt Hire using our standard hire jackets and accessories.

As we are making a kilt for you, you wont be able to try on the exact tartan of your choice.

Once the kilt has been made we will contact you to organise a Full Try On appointment.

Childs Kilt Outfit Measurement

At this 30 minute appointment we will be measuring your child for their kilt hire.

They will not get the opportunity to try on the full outfit that they will be hiring.

We will get the measurements we require using the following process;

Kilt

We will try a kilt on top of your childs trousers to get the correct size.

We may ask your child to try it on in the fitting room if necessary.

The kilt may not be the tartan that they will be hiring.

Jacket & Waistcoat

We will try a jacket and waistcoat on your child to get the correct size.

The jacket may not be the color that they will be hiring.

The jacket and waistcoat come as a set, so they will be the same size.

If different sized jacket & waistcoat are required, there will be a £20 surcharge.

Shoes

Your child will try a pair of shoes on to get the correct size.

Our shoes are made quite big, so it is unlikely that they will match their current shoe size.

It is very important that they try these on at the appointment.

Shirt

If they require a shirt, you will try one on for size.

As we do not know your sizes when you first visit us, we cannot guarantee that you will be able to try on the outfit at this type of appointment.

Because of this, we would advise not bringing additional family members who want to see your child in their outfit.

To try on the full outfit we need to organise a Full Try On appointment.

Additional family members who would like to see your child in his outfit are more than welcome at the Full Try On appointment.

Remeasure

At this 15 minute appointment, you will be going through the same process as the original Kilt Measurement.

We recommend a remeasure appointment approximately 4-6 week prior to collection.

But if you feel that you have lost or gained any weight since your original fitting appointment,
please arrange a remeasure as soon as you can.

Remeasure

At this 30 minute appointment, you will be going through the same process as the original Childs Measurement.

We recommend a remeasure appointment approximately 4 weeks prior to collection.

However, if you feel that your child grown since your original fitting appointment,
please arrange a remeasure as soon as you can.

Please be aware that these outfits are limited due to demand, and if your child’s sizes have changed,
we cannot be held responsible if we do not have any outfits available in the size or style that you desire.

NO APPOINTMENT

Turning Up Without An Appointment

Booking an appointment is advisable no matter which day you visit us.

CUSTOMERS WITH APPOINTMENTS TAKE PRIORITY OVER CUSTOMERS THAT DONT!

Please do not turn up without an appointment to avoid disappointment.

What we will do

If you turn up to one of our stores without an appointment, we will do our best to serve you.

You may get served immediately, but you may need to wait if we have other customers.

If we are fully booked at the time you visit us, we will try to see you later in the day.

If we are fully booked for the rest of the day, we will arrange an appointment for another day.

Respect in the Workplace

We expect our staff to be able to do their job without feeling intimidated.

We ask all our customer to treat our staff with respect at all times.

Customers under the influence of alcohol may not be served.

Customers not complying with these rules will be asked to leave the premises.

Failure to comply with these requests may result in hire booking being cancelled.
